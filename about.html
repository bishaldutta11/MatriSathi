The Genesis of Matri Sathi

Parenthood is a beautiful journey, but it is often accompanied by sleepless nights and constant worry. We realized that traditional baby monitors were passive—they only showed you what was happening, relying entirely on the parent's vigilance.

We built Matri Sathi to be an active guardian. By utilizing state-of-the-art computer vision and audio classification models, Matri Sathi doesn't just watch; it understands. It can differentiate between a cry of hunger and a cry of pain. It knows when a sleeping position becomes unsafe.

Our goal isn't to replace parents, but to give them a reliable, untiring partner that never blinks.

Our Core Values

Absolute Privacy
