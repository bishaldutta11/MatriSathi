Need Probability Breakdown

Diagnostic Guide

🍼
Hunger
Rhythmic, repetitive crying, often paired with sucking sounds or hand-to-mouth motions.
🫄
Pain / Colic
Sudden, intense, high-pitched shrieks. Baby may draw legs up or arch their back.
😴
Tiredness
Whiny, nasal cry, gradually building. Accompanied by eye rubbing or yawning.
🧷
Discomfort / Wet Diaper
Fussy, grumbly cry. Usually caused by a wet/soiled diaper or uncomfortable clothing.
💨
Gas / Needs Burping
Flatulence or bloating. Accompanied by grunting, squirming, or release of air bubbles.
🧸
Boredom / Seeking Attention
Monotone, calling cry. Stops when picked up, cuddled, or engaged with a toy.
🙀
Fear / Startled
Sudden screaming outburst following loud noises, bright lights, or sudden movements.
🌡️
Temperature (Too Hot/Cold)
Shivering whimpers (if cold) or sweaty, flushed fussiness (if too hot).
🤒
Sickness / Fever
Weak, hoarse, low-energy crying. Persistent distress that is difficult to soothe.
⚡
Frustration / Overstimulation
Frustrated screeching. Baby turning away from sights/sounds. Needs calm space.
